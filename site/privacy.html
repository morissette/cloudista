1. Overview

Cloudista ("we", "us", or "our") operates cloudista.org (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. By using the Site or submitting your email address, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Email address — when you submit the "Notify Me" form on the Site.

Information collected automatically

When you visit the Site or submit the form, our servers automatically receive:

  • IP address — used for basic spam and abuse prevention.
  • User-agent string — the browser/device identifier sent by your browser.
  • Referrer URL and page request logs — standard web server access logs retained for up to 30 days.

We do not use third-party analytics scripts, advertising trackers, or fingerprinting on this Site.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To send you a one-time email confirmation to verify your subscription.
  • To notify you when Cloudista launches.
  • To prevent duplicate or fraudulent sign-ups.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We will not use your email address for any other purpose without your explicit consent.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use Amazon Web Services (AWS Simple Email Service) to send transactional emails. AWS processes your email address on our behalf under their Privacy Policy. No other third parties receive your data.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal process.
  • Business transfer: If Cloudista is acquired or merged, subscriber data may be transferred as part of that transaction. You will be notified before your data is subject to a different privacy policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe, your record is marked as inactive and purged from our active mailing list. Anonymised aggregate statistics (e.g. total subscriber counts) may be retained indefinitely.

6. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, including encrypted HTTPS connections, restricted database access, and per-subscriber tokens for confirmation and unsubscribe links. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, or delete it. To exercise any of these rights, or to unsubscribe at any time, contact us at admin@cloudista.org. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

The Site does not set any cookies or use local storage. We do not use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or any persistent tracking technologies.

9. Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If the changes are material, we will notify subscribers by email.
